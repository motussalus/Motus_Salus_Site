Rural Culture

Rural culture and clinical care

Most of the longer rural culture writing will live on Substack. This page will serve as a simple index for selected articles, essays, and teaching materials that are especially relevant to clinicians, students, and behavioral health professionals working with rural communities.

Topic 01

Origins of rural culture

Essays on the historical roots of rural identity, including land, labor, settlement, family systems, faith, independence, and the cultural memory that continues to shape rural communities.

Topic 02

Distrust and help-seeking

Writing on why some rural clients avoid formal systems, delay care, or expect judgment from institutions, and how clinicians can build trust without treating rural clients as a stereotype.

Read on Substack

Topic 03

Work, stigma, and mental health

Reflections on occupational identity, privacy, self-reliance, masculinity, reputation, and the fear that seeking therapy may affect work, standing, or trust in the community.
