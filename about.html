About

Practical work at the intersection of movement, mental health, and rural care.

Motus Salus is a professional home for clinical tools, writing, and educational projects that translate exercise science, clinical psychology, and cultural context into practical behavioral health resources.

Exercise and behavioral health

A central focus of this work is the development of clearer ways to describe and apply exercise as a clinical tool, including the 7 Specifier Framework for Exercise Prescription and the Exercise Px project.

Rural culture and clinical education

Motus Salus also houses writing on rural identity, rural mental health, and the cultural assumptions that can shape clinical care, trust, stigma, and help-seeking.

Useful, restrained, and practical

The design goal is simple: build resources that are clear enough to use, honest about their limits, and practical enough to support clinicians, students, researchers, and community partners.
