About

The idea behind Motus Salus

Motus Salus began as the dream of researcher and clinician Spencer Hills. In preparation for presenting a poster at the Society of Behavioral Medicine Conference in April 2026, Spencer created ExercisePx.com to help people understand the 7 Specifier Framework for Exercise Prescription, a framework he developed from his education in exercise science and behavioral health.

After building Exercise Px, he decided he did not want to stop with one idea. Motus Salus was formed to continue developing practical tools, writing, and projects that bring useful innovation into the field of behavioral health.

Exercise Px

Exercise Px is the first major project under Motus Salus. It focuses on helping clinicians, students, and researchers think more precisely about exercise as a clinical intervention.

Rural culture

Motus Salus also serves as a home for writing on rural culture, rural mental health, and the ways culture, work, land, faith, family, and stigma can shape clinical care.

The broader goal

The purpose of Motus Salus is to keep building clear, practical, and honest resources that help translate ideas into tools people can actually use.
